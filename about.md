    <h1 class="chapter__title">About Trellis</h1>
  </header>

  <div class="chapter__body">
    <p>Trellis is a framework for AI adoption in organizations starting from zero — no AI initiatives in flight, no data infrastructure decisions made, no governance structures defined, just a desire to begin.</p>

    <p>It assumes that the right structures, policies, and metrics cannot be designed in advance. They have to be built up from real project experience. Trellis exists to keep the right questions visible without forcing premature answers, and to give leaders a way to start moving while the picture is still incomplete.</p>
